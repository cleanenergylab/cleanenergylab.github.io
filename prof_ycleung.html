Bio

Professor Leung is now a full professor and head of the Department of Mechanical Engineering specializing in environmental pollution control and renewable & clean energy development. He has published more than 500 articles in this area including 330+ peer reviewed top SCI journal papers. He was invited to publish more than 20 highly cited review articles in leading energy and environment related journals. His current h-index is 80 with total citations 36000+. He is one of the top 1% highly cited scientists in the world in energy field since 2010 (Essential Science Indicators) and named as a Highly Cited Researcher by Clarivate Analytics from 2017-2021. Prof. Leung has delivered more than 80 keynote and invited speeches in many conferences as well as public lectures.
